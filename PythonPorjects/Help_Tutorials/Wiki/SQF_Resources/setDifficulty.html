    setDifficulty


    The command is deprecated in VBS4 24.1+. Use setSetting instead.

    Introduced in

    Version:

    3.4

    Description

    Description:

    Controls specific simulation options (difficulty settings) for the current user.

    The following consideration apply:

    • In VBS4 23.2+, the command effect is immediate (no need to restart the mission). In pre-VBS4 23.2 releases, after executing the command, restart the mission for the command to take effect.

    • The new settings are permanent (until set to a different value), and are visible in the options dialog.

    • In VBS4 23.2+, the 3rdPersonView simulation option is called ThirdPersonView.

    The options are:

    • Weapons:

      • realisticGunnery - Advanced Gunnery Features (realistic gunnery simulation, gunner view is removed from Commander optics, disables reload when weapon is loaded).

      • autoAim - Auto-Aim (assists aiming by locking the weapon cursor onto the target after aiming at it, indicated by a color change from white to dark red).

      • autoGuideAT - Auto-Guide AT (allows guided missiles to be treated as "fire and forget").

      • constantTurretSlewRate - Constant Turret Slew Rate (turret has the same turn rate irrespective of the turret zoom).

      • ManualFire - Manual Fire (enables the "Manual fire" user action).

      • TransparentPass3 - Semi-Transparent Aiming (simulates aiming with both eyes open by making weapon semi-transparent).

      • grenadeTrajectory - Show Grenade Trajectory (shows the approximate trajectory line for both throwing and launching grenades).

      • weaponCursor - Weapon Crosshairs (toggles virtual crosshairs, providing an approximation of where the weapon muzzle is pointing).

      • safetyOn - Weapon Safety Switch (activates the weapon safety mode, default: ON).

      • MouseLook - Mouse Free Look (free look by using the mouse).

      • NVGwithWeapScope - Force NVG Compatibility.

    • HUD:

      • SoldierNames - 3D Soldier Names (shows the names and health bars of soldiers in your team).

      • clockIndicator - Clock Indicator (provides a "Clock method" of target indication, only appears when an enemy is located).

      • enemyTag - Enemy TAG (shows the name and health bar of a hostile target, if your crosshairs are aimed at it).

      • hud - Extended HUD Info (indicates the location and estimated distance of enemy forces, the information is available from all friendly sources and appears automatically on your screen).

      • hudPerm - Extended HUD Info Always On (shows extended HUD info persistently, rather than fading in and out).

      • map - Extended Map Info (shows real-time positions of known friendly/hostile units on the map).

      • friendlyTag - Friendly TAG (this shows the name and health bars of a friendly target, if your crosshairs are aimed at it).

      • hideUI - Hide UI (removes unit info resources such as: health bar, radar, weapon cursor, etc; forced in MP).

      • hudWp - HUD Waypoints Info (waypoints are shown "floating" above their location with an icon and distance indicator).

      • hudWpPerm - HUD Waypoints Info Always On (the next waypoint is persistently shown, rather than fading in and out).

      • allowHUDLabels - HUD Labels (turns on HUD labels for everything, except player-controlled avatars).

      • visualDirection - Nearby Shot Awareness (visualize nearby shot directions).

      • showSpeakerIcons - Show Speaker Icons in AAR (displays speaker icons in the timeline during AAR recordings).

      • ShowArmAMap - Use Game Map (shows the old-style ArmA game map, instead of the new C2 interface).

      • VehicleAwareness - Vehicle Awareness Indicator (enables/disables display of situation-awareness indicator for vehicles).

      • NoParaglideHUD - Hide Steerable Parachute HUD (hides special HUD controls shown when the player is in a steerable parachute).

      • InfantryHeading - Infantry Heading Indicator (heading indicator for infantry).

      • AdvancedMinimap - Advanced Mini-Map (replace mini-map with advanced one).

    • Multiplayer:

      • canHost - Can Host MP Session (allows users to host multiplayer Sessions).

      • AllowSeagull - Seagull Respawn (enables a dead player to respawn as a seagull to continue observing the battlefield).

      • netStats - Show Network Stats (activates the network statistics display, accessible to players during a network session).

      • vonId - VON ID (shows VOIP ID).

      • startAARInMultiplayer - Start AAR in Multiplayer (automatically starts AAR recording during MP sessions).

    • Simulation:

      • AdvancedHeadInjury - Advanced Head Injury (head injuries can cause dizziness and impaired vision).

      • Wound - Advanced Wounding and Amputations (simulates injuries of specific limbs, including amputations and bleeding).

      • AnalogThrottle - Analog Throttle (enables the use of an analog throttle). Removed in VBS3 22.1+ and VBS4 22.2+.

      • BackblastDamage - Backblast Damage.

      • Bleeding - Bleeding (units may die of bleeding).

      • showHints - Cadet Mode and Training Hints (simplifies user interface, toggles the visibility of hints for specific items).

      • SuppressPlayer - Combat User Suppression (the player can be effectively suppressed by incoming fire - white flashes and momentary loss of focus).

      • armor - Extended Armor (simulates the addition of extra armor plates to armored vehicles, allows the vehicle to sustain more damage before total destruction).

      • HealthDegrad - Health Degradation (health degrades when less than 75%, and stops at 10%).

      • FollowContour - Helicopter Follow Contour (forces the helicopter to gain/decrease altitude according to terrain contours, adopting a "tactical flight" protocol).

      • HitBodyEffect - Hit Body Effect.

      • TremblingHands - Injured Hands Trembling (hands damage affects aiming).

      • OneIncapacitatedScreen - One Incapacitated Screen (same text on the black screen for player death and unconsciousness).

      • RealisticFatigue - Realistic Fatigue (morale is effected by physical stresses, such as weight being carried, wounds and the length of time in battle).

      • RealisticRepairs - Realistic Repairs (repairs take longer time and players have to get out of repair trucks to carry them out).

      • SimplifiedHeliModel - Simple Helicopter Model (simplifies helicopter flight physics, disables crashing and speed/elevation are controlled by single key presses).

      • unlimitedSaves - Unlimited Saves (allows an unlimited number of single player mission saves).

      • UnlimitedFuel - Unlimited Fuel (allows vehicles to never run out of fuel).

      • VehicleQualification - Vehicle and Weapon Qualifications (user capabilities reduce vehicle and weapon usage by its qualification).

      • FootprintRendering (VBS4 22.2+) - Rendering of footprints on the terrain.

      • InversedShipControl (VBS4 22.2+) - Reverses ship-steering control.

      • LifeformSnowCompression (VBS4 23.2+) - Snow compression caused by lifeforms.

      • WheelSnowCompression (VBS4 23.2+) - Snow compression caused by wheeled vehicles.

    • AI:

      • AIVoices - AI Voices (transmits all AI radio traffic).

      • autoSpot - Auto-Report (your character automatically radios in reports).

      • SurrenderBLUFOR - BLUFOR Surrender (BLUFOR surrenders if conditions are met, requires morale simulation to be enabled).

      • SurrenderIndependent - INDEPENDENT Surrender (Independent surrenders if conditions are met, requires morale simulation to be enabled).

      • LongTermMorale - Long Term Morale (slows the effects of morale fluctuations, by evaluating the moral of a unit over 60 second intervals, instead of 1 sec).

      • Morale - Morale Simulation (activates the morale system, strong and long-term morale are dependent on this setting).

      • SurrenderOPFOR - OPFOR Surrender (OPFOR surrenders if conditions are met, requires morale simulation to be enabled).

      • StrongMorale - Strong Morale (morale has a stronger impact on AI skills and conduct in battle).

      • XaitmentInBuildings - Xaitment Pathplanning Inside Buildings (enables Xaitment pathplanning for all units inside objects with an attached navigation mesh).

      • ultraAI - Super AI (increases the intelligence of the AI, which performs with a higher degree of cunning and awareness).

    • Camera:

      • 3rdPersonView (VBS4 23.2+: ThirdPersonView) - Third-Person View (enables or disables third-person view).

      • autoVehicleCameraTurn - Automatic Driver Camera Turn (if true, the view direction is affected by the direction the vehicle is headed in; if false, the camera is independent of vehicle steering).

      • rteCamAbsolute - 3D Editor Camera Retains Absolute Height (allows the editor camera in the 3D editor to follow an absolute height, instead of a relative height).

      • showAvatar - Show Avatar in 1st Person View (draws the avatar in 1st-person view, when in vehicle).

      • useCameraDOF - Use DOF for Optics (enables the default ArmA Depth-of-Field effect for optics).

      • hudGroupInfo - HUD Show Group.

      • PeripheralVision - Adds visual signals to characters who are slightly out of direct view.

    Syntax

    Syntax:

    setDifficulty [option, setting, force]

    Parameters:

    • option: String - Option name (see description).

    • setting: Boolean - New value for difficulty setting.

    • force: Boolean - Sets the "Enforce" (by admin) option.

    Return Value:

    Nothing

    Examples

    Examples:

    		 
    // Pre-VBS4 23.2
    setDifficulty ["3rdPersonView", false, false];

    // VBS4 23.2+
    setDifficulty ["ThirdPersonView", false, false];
